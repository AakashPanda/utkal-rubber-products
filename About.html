Our Story

A Legacy of Quality Since 1996

Utkal Rubber Products came into being in the year 1996 as if to signal the dawn of a dazzling new era in Rubber moulding with regard to a technological revolution, the like of which rubber moulding industry had ever experienced before.

The founder, lost no time in realising that the success of their enterprise depended as much, or rather even more, upon the quality, consistency and reliability of the range of products, solutions and their services they offered; also, ensured that they strictly adhered to this 'principle' throughout their journey spanning over 2 decade, to be where they are today.

Today, we stand as a trusted partner for industries across India, delivering premium rubber moulding and sealing solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.
